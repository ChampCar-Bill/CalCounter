Camera Barcode Scanner

Scan a food barcode with your phone camera or type the barcode manually. Nutrition data comes from the Open Food Facts database. Scan once and save products to Favorites for faster entry later.

Works best on Android Chrome. Manual barcode lookup works as a backup.

Meal Templates

Save frequently eaten meals as templates. A template can contain multiple foods and can be added to your daily log with a single tap.

Built-In Food Search

Search common foods already included in the app and load their nutrition values into the food form.

Quick Estimate

Type common foods such as "2 eggs, banana, oatmeal" and the app will estimate calories and nutrition using its built-in food database.

Favorite Foods

Favorites are individual foods you eat often. Add them quickly without searching or scanning again.
